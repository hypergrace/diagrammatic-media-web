I designed PCBs based on a schematic design by Ray Wilson. The intention of the workshop was to introduce beginners to DIY synth-making with hands on soldering and live performance at the end.

You can find the workshop materials here.

I ran this workshop at Temple University's Electronics Faire in April 2024. The workshop is now part of CCAM's Estuary group repertoire, which means it now serves as a way for new teachers to learn how to teach these materials.
