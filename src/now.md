## NOW

### Fall 2025/Winter 2026

Invited responded to Indiana University Bloomington's Digital Art MFA portfolio review.

Performing live, real-time spatialized experimental computer music as HYPERGRACE in March at Elastic Arts at the [CLEAT SERIES](https://elasticarts.org/cleat) on 16 channel bespoke sound system.

Performed live experimental computer music as HYPERGRACE among 20 performance artists at THRESHOLD, a durational performance art event curated by Claire Fleming Staples at a budding project space in Humboldt Park.
